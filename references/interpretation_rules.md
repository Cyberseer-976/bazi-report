# Interpretation Rules

- Treat Ba Zi as traditional cultural analysis and reflective language.
- Prefer "倾向", "可能", "较容易", "适合关注" over absolute statements.
- Start with the day master, month branch, element balance, and ten-god structure.
- Do not over-weight single symbols, isolated 神煞, or one clash/combination.
- If birth time is unknown, avoid firm conclusions about children, later-life patterns, hour-pillar ten gods, and detailed career timing.
- If the birth date is close to a solar term boundary, call out that year/month pillars may need high-precision ephemeris verification.
- Do not make claims about lifespan, death, severe illness, unavoidable disaster, guaranteed wealth, guaranteed marriage, or exam outcomes.
- For health, mention lifestyle tendencies only; advise professional help for real symptoms.
- For finance, discuss risk preference and resource management only; do not give investment advice.
- For relationships, focus on communication needs and recurring patterns; do not frame compatibility as fate.
- When explaining 喜忌, describe it as a tendency inferred from chart structure, not as a final verdict.
