# Terminology

- 四柱: 年柱、月柱、日柱、时柱.
- 八字: Four pillars represented by eight characters: heavenly stem and earthly branch for each pillar.
- 日主: The heavenly stem of the day pillar; the center of interpretation.
- 天干: 甲乙丙丁戊己庚辛壬癸.
- 地支: 子丑寅卯辰巳午未申酉戌亥.
- 五行: 木、火、土、金、水.
- 十神: 比肩、劫财、食神、伤官、偏财、正财、七杀、正官、偏印、正印.
- 藏干: Heavenly stems stored inside earthly branches.
- 合冲刑害破: Branch interactions; use as structural signals, not standalone verdicts.
- 大运: Ten-year luck cycles determined by birth month pillar, gender, and solar-term proximity (节). Direction (顺排/逆排) depends on birth year's yin/yang and gender. Starting age = days from birth to next/previous 节 ÷ 3. Each 大运 pillar spans 10 years.
- 流年: Annual pillar influence based on the calendar year's stem-branch combination (公历年). Each year's stem forms a ten-god relationship with the day master.
