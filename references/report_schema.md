# Report Schema

Use this structure for standard Chinese Ba Zi reports.

1. 基本排盘
   - 出生信息
   - 年柱、月柱、日柱、时柱
   - 日主、五行分布、十神摘要
2. 日主强弱
   - State whether 身强 or 身弱 based on diagnostics data.
   - Show reasoning: 得令/得地/得助 + 同党异党比例.
   - This is the foundation for 用神 and 格局 analysis.
3. 用神喜忌
   - Based on 强弱, identify which five elements are helpful (喜) and which are burdensome (忌).
   - Reference which helpful elements already appear in the chart.
   - Use measured language: "适合以X为用", not absolute claims.
4. 格局分析
   - Present 格局 candidates from month-branch hidden stems.
   - Discuss whether the 格局 is formed (成格) or not, given the 强弱.
   - After analysis: 格局点睛 box summarizing all notable chart patterns (财多身弱, 官印相生, 食神制杀, etc.) as tagged highlights.
5. 地支关系
   - List 合/冲/刑/害 among the four branches.
   - Map each to its 柱 position for life-domain context.
6. 五行结构
   - Describe abundant, moderate, and weak elements.
   - Explain what each element represents in ordinary language.
7. 十神关系
   - Explain visible stems first, then hidden stems.
   - Describe behavioral tendencies and resource patterns.
8. 性格与行为模式
   - Use balanced language: strengths, blind spots, adjustment methods.
9. 事业与工作方式
   - Discuss suitable environments, collaboration style, and decision rhythm.
10. 财务与资源管理
    - Discuss money behavior and risk awareness. Do not promise wealth.
11. 关系与情感模式
    - Discuss communication style and relationship needs. Do not predict divorce or marriage certainty.
12. 生活节律与健康提示
    - Keep this general: sleep, stress, movement, routine. Do not diagnose disease.
13. 大运/流年
    - 大运方向 (顺排/逆排), 起运年龄, and starting year
    - Current or targeted 大运 pillar with ten god and element
    - Requested 流年 pillars with stems, branches, elements, and ten gods
    - Cross-reference: which 大运 pillar each 流年 falls under
    - Relationship between 大运 ten god, 流年 ten god, and natal chart patterns
    - General stage-of-life tendencies and annual themes (not specific predictions)
    - Include only when supported by reliable inputs (gender provided for 大运)
14. 综合建议
    - Give practical suggestions tied to chart tendencies.
15. 古今参照
    - Match the chart's temperament to a historical figure.
    - List resonance points grounded in actual chart features.
    - Use measured language: temperament analogy, not literal reincarnation.

附录：免责声明

    - This section is unnumbered in the rendered report.
